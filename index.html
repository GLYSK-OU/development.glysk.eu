The Development Center builds for clients — and for GLYSK. AnglerBook is our own product: a native iPhone and Apple Watch app, taken from first sketch to the App Store in-house. It is the clearest proof of what the team can ship.

AnglerBook

A career-long log for fly fishing — every session, fly and fish, mapped and remembered.

iPhone · Apple Watch  —  In beta · Coming to the App Store

A wrist-first capture flow lets anglers start and mark a session without taking the phone out. GPS sessions map the water, a living gear catalog tracks the rods, lines and flies that worked, and waterside weather plus Apple Fitness data fold into each record automatically. Live Activities keep an active session on the Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island, and optional iCloud sync keeps the whole log private and consistent across devices — with a subscription tier built on StoreKit.

SwiftUI · watchOS · MapKit · HealthKit · WeatherKit · CloudKit · StoreKit · Live Activities
